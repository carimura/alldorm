AllDorm Inc. Company Overview

AllDorm Inc. is quickly establishing itself as the industry leader in providing products and services to college students nationwide.

Since its conception in January of 2000, AllDorm Inc. has successfully established its reputation by providing an innovative and exciting product line that satisfies a college students' every need. AllDorm is dedicated to making the college experience even more enjoyable.

The Santa Clara, California based company began in the dorm rooms of Santa Clara University when four entrepreneurial students realized there was no centralized business that met their needs as consumers. Since then, Ryan Garman, co-founder of AllDorm Inc., has recruited some of the Bay Area's brightest and most enterprising professionals to develop a company whose sole goal is to meet the product needs of college students in the U.S. The company's management team has its finger on the pulse of today's college market allowing it to identify youth consumer trends as they develop and direct its tactical planning accordingly, making AllDorm the ultimate college everything superstore.

Ryan Garman - Director & CEO

Known for his intelligence and sense of humor, Ryan Garman is the quintessential CEO. Ryan went through the painful and laborious process of buying everything he needed for his new life at Santa Clara University and then moved everything cross-country in a rented U-Haul truck. Birthed from this pain, AllDorm was designed to make the transition from home-life to college-life as easy and fun as possible. Ryan then assembled the team and obtained the resources to make his vision a reality. Today Ryan leads the company as it aims at accomplishing one simple goal: to enhance the residential life of college students nationwide.

Prior to co-founding AllDorm, Ryan was the CEO of a regional home furnishings company. When he's not managing the day-to-day operations of AllDorm, you'll find Ryan pole-vaulting, cooking, or traveling.

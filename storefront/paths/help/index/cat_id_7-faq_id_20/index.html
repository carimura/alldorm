How do I place an item into my Shopping Cart?
In order to purchase a product at Alldorm, it must first be placed in a Shopping Cart. This acts as a temporary storage space for the product while you continue shopping. Simply click the ’Add to Shopping Cart’ button on the product page to place the product in the cart. You can then continue shopping or proceed to the checkout process. To view your Shopping Cart, simply click the ’Shopping Cart’ link on the top right of the page.
Each product page has an Add to Cart button to place items in your Cart.



Your shopping cart can contain as many products as you want to order. You can change your quantity of a specific product in the shopping cart by clicking the update button. You may also remove items by clicking the remove button. You may continue shopping by using any navigation features on the page or by clicking the Continue Shopping button. Once you are satisfied with your shopping cart, click the Proceed to Checkout button to finalize your order. Have all your shipping and billing information ready before you enter checkout.

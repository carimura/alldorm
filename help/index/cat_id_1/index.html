Help / Ordering

Are there any other ways to order besides online?
AllDorm also offers a mail catalog in addition to the online store. You can request a catalog by calling 1-866-ALLDORM or through your account. You can use the catalog to pay by check or money order. You may also call us to place an order over the phone. To place an order over the phone, be sure to know the products you want, and to have all your billing and shipping information ready.
 
Can I return a product if I am unsatisfied with my purchase?
The 10/30 Guarantee
We understand how hectic college life can be and realize just how important it is to be flexible. If you are not completely satisfied with one of our products, you can send it back for a full refund within 10 days of delivery. A credit will be offered if the product is returned between 10 and 30 days of delivery. Both open and unopened packages will be accepted.

AllDorm will gladly refund the cost of shipping for returns due to our error. Simply contact our Returns Department and we will have the product picked up. However, please understand that AllDorm will not be held responsible for the cost of shipping under any other circumstances.

  1. Fulfilling a Return
    In every package received there should be a return slip containing an address for the AllDorm Returns Department. Follow the detailed instructions on the returns slip and fill-in all relevant information. Please note that only once the Customer Service Representative receives the return with this slip will your refund be processed.


  2. Return Processing
    Once the Returns Department receives the product, a Customer Service Representative will analyze your return and the circumstances surrounding your return, and will determine the appropriate refund. We will then notify you via e-mail.


  3. Contacting us
    Please note we assure a speedy turnaround time from the moment we receive the return. To check the status of a return, you can contact our service department by clicking here.


 
Can you walk me through the checkout process?
AllDorm's checkout process can be completed in a few short steps. Once you are satisfied with your shopping cart, simply click checkout on the top right of any page. Be sure to have all shipping and billing information ready to speed up the process.
